## Econometrics & Data

I use **Stata** as my primary tool for panel econometrics and gravity model estimation. **R** (with `fixest` and `ggplot2`) handles high-dimensional fixed effects and publication-quality figures. **Python** covers data scraping, text analysis, and automation.

For data, I rely primarily on WRDS/Compustat for firm-level data, UN Comtrade for trade flows, and the CEPII gravity dataset.

## Writing & Publishing

All academic papers are typeset in **LaTeX** via Overleaf for real-time collaboration. References are managed through **Zotero** (collection) and BibTeX (LaTeX integration). Research notes and outlines live in **Obsidian**.
