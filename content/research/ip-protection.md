# Intellectual Property Protection

## Research Overview

Intellectual property institutions influence innovation incentives, market competition, and technology diffusion.

This research area explores how legal protection mechanisms affect economic outcomes and innovation performance.

---

## Core Questions

- Does stronger intellectual property protection stimulate innovation?

- How does judicial enforcement influence firm behavior?

- What is the relationship between patents and technology transfer?

- How should intellectual property systems balance innovation and access?

---

## Research Topics

### Patent Protection

Examining patent systems and innovation incentives.

### Judicial Enforcement

Studying how courts influence intellectual property effectiveness.

### Innovation Policy

Analyzing the interaction between law and economic growth.
