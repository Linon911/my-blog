## Overview

Povaka Research Platform is an ongoing project aimed at building a modern academic website and knowledge management system. The platform integrates research projects, publications, reading databases, and academic resources into a unified digital workspace.

## Objectives

1. Organize research outputs and working papers.
2. Maintain a structured reading database.
3. Develop an academic portfolio and curriculum vitae system.
4. Improve research visibility and discoverability.
5. Build a long-term scholarly knowledge infrastructure.

## Current Status

Ongoing Development

## Key Features

* Research Database
* Publications Management
* Reading Notes System
* Academic Timeline
* Curriculum Vitae
* Knowledge Organization Framework
