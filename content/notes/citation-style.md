# Citation Style

## Journal Article

Author. (Year).

Title.

Journal.

Volume(Issue), Pages.

---

Example:

Acemoglu, D. (2002).

Technical Change, Inequality, and the Labor Market.

Journal of Economic Literature, 40(1), 7–72.

---

## Book

Author. (Year).

Title.

Publisher.

---

Example:

North, D. C. (1990).

Institutions, Institutional Change and Economic Performance.

Cambridge University Press.
